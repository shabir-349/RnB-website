Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 2026  ·  R&B — Research and Beyond

1. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information necessary to operate R&B. This includes:

  • Account information: Your full name and email address, provided when you register.
  • Payment information: Screenshots of payment transactions that you upload to verify your subscription. We do not collect or store card numbers, bank account details, or any raw financial credentials.
  • Usage data: Basic session information (login timestamps, page interactions) collected automatically by Supabase as part of authentication.

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as health information, government ID numbers, or precise location data.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to:

  • Manage your account: Create and authenticate your session, display your name in the dashboard, and manage your subscription plan.
  • Verify payments: Review the screenshot you upload to confirm your payment before granting access to paid content.
  • Send email notifications: Notify you when your payment is approved or rejected, and send any important account updates. Transactional emails are sent via Brevo.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored and managed through Supabase, a managed open-source backend platform. This includes:

  • User account records (name, email, plan) stored in a PostgreSQL database.
  • Payment screenshots stored in Supabase Storage (a secure object storage service).
  • Authentication sessions managed by Supabase Auth.

Supabase infrastructure may be hosted in the United States or European Union. By using R&B, you consent to your data being processed in these regions.

4. Third-Party Services

R&B uses the following third-party services to operate the platform:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and file storage (Privacy Policy)
  • Vercel — website hosting and deployment (Privacy Policy)
  • Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) — transactional email delivery (Privacy Policy)
  • EmailJS — supplementary email sending from the browser (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Fonts — web font delivery. Google may log font requests (Privacy Policy)

Each service operates under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review these policies.

5. Cookies

R&B uses a small number of cookies and browser storage mechanisms:

  • Session cookies: Set by Supabase to maintain your authentication state. These are essential for you to stay logged in and cannot be disabled without breaking core functionality.
  • localStorage: Used to remember your theme preference (dark or light mode). No personal data is stored here.

We do not use advertising cookies, third-party tracking pixels, or behavioural analytics services.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account remains active. If you request account deletion, we will remove your profile and associated data within a reasonable time.

Payment records (including uploaded screenshots) may be retained for up to 2 years for accounting, compliance, and dispute resolution purposes, even after account deletion.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your account and associated personal data.
  • Objection: Object to certain uses of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at researchandbeyondd@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Contact

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please reach out:

  • Email: researchandbeyondd@gmail.com
